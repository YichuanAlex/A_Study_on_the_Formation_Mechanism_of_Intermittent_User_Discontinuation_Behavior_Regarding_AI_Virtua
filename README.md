## Overview | 项目概述

**English:**  
This research project explores the formation mechanism of intermittent discontinuation behavior among users of AI virtual librarians in public libraries. Using grounded theory and fuzzy-set Qualitative Comparative Analysis (fsQCA), we investigate the complex interplay of factors that lead users to temporarily cease using AI librarian services despite initial adoption.

**中文:**  
本研究项目探讨公共图书馆 AI 虚拟馆员用户间歇性中辍行为的形成机理。基于扎根理论和模糊集定性比较分析 (fsQCA) 方法，我们研究了导致用户在初次采用后暂时停止使用 AI 馆员服务的复杂因素交互作用。

---

## Research Background | 研究背景

**English:**  
With the rapid development of artificial intelligence technology, public libraries worldwide have introduced AI virtual librarians to enhance service efficiency and user experience. However, practical operations reveal a common phenomenon: many users exhibit "intermittent discontinuation behavior" - they adopt the service initially, then discontinue use, and may resume later. This behavior pattern differs significantly from traditional continuous discontinuation, yet its formation mechanism remains unclear.

**中文:**  
随着人工智能技术的快速发展，全球公共图书馆纷纷引入 AI 虚拟馆员以提升服务效率和用户体验。然而，实际运营中发现一个普遍现象：许多用户表现出"间歇性中辍行为"——他们最初采用服务，然后停止使用，之后可能再次恢复使用。这种行为模式与传统的持续性中辍有显著差异，但其形成机理尚不明确。

---

## Research Objectives | 研究目标

**English:**
1. Identify the key factors influencing intermittent discontinuation behavior of AI virtual librarian users
2. Reveal the formation mechanism and causal pathways of such behavior using grounded theory
3. Identify multiple equivalent configurations leading to intermittent discontinuation through fsQCA
4. Provide theoretical support and practical recommendations for public libraries to optimize AI virtual librarian services

**中文:**
1. 识别影响 AI 虚拟馆员用户间歇性中辍行为的关键因素
2. 运用扎根理论揭示间歇性中辍行为的形成机理和因果路径
3. 通过 fsQCA 识别导致间歇性中辍的多种等效组态
4. 为公共图书馆优化 AI 虚拟馆员服务提供理论支持和实践建议

## Methodology | 研究方法

### 1. Grounded Theory | 扎根理论

**English:**
- **Data Collection**: In-depth interviews with 30 users who have experienced intermittent discontinuation
- **Three-Level Coding**:
  - Open Coding: Identifying initial concepts from interview data
  - Axial Coding: Establishing relationships between categories
  - Selective Coding: Developing core categories and theoretical framework
- **Theoretical Saturation Test**: Ensuring no new concepts emerge from additional data

**中文:**
- **数据收集**: 对 30 位经历过间歇性中辍的用户进行深度访谈
- **三级编码**:
  - 开放式编码：从访谈数据中识别初始概念
  - 主轴编码：建立范畴间的关系
  - 选择性编码：发展核心范畴和理论框架
- **理论饱和度检验**: 确保额外数据不再产生新范畴

### 2. Fuzzy-Set Qualitative Comparative Analysis | 模糊集定性比较分析

**English:**
- **Sample Size**: 150 valid questionnaire responses
- **Condition Variables**:
  - Service Quality (SQ)
  - Perceived Usefulness (PU)
  - Perceived Ease of Use (PEOU)
  - Privacy Concerns (PC)
  - Alternative Attractiveness (AA)
  - Switching Costs (SC)
- **Outcome Variable**: Intermittent Discontinuation Behavior (IDB)
- **Analysis Steps**: Necessity analysis → Truth table construction → Configuration analysis → Robustness testing

**中文:**
- **样本量**: 150 份有效问卷
- **条件变量**:
  - 服务质量 (SQ)
  - 感知有用性 (PU)
  - 感知易用性 (PEOU)
  - 隐私关注 (PC)
  - 替代吸引力 (AA)
  - 转换成本 (SC)
- **结果变量**: 间歇性中辍行为 (IDB)
- **分析步骤**: 必要性分析 → 真值表构建 → 组态分析 → 稳健性检验

## Key Findings | 主要发现

**English:**
1. **Multiple Causal Pathways**: Intermittent discontinuation behavior results from multiple equivalent configurations rather than a single factor
2. **Core Conditions**: Low service quality and high privacy concerns are core conditions in most configurations
3. **Asymmetric Relationships**: Factors leading to discontinuation differ from those influencing continuation
4. **Contextual Sensitivity**: The effect of certain factors varies depending on user characteristics and usage scenarios
5. **Practical Implications**: Libraries should adopt differentiated strategies based on different user groups

**中文:**
1. **多重因果路径**: 间歇性中辍行为由多种等效组态导致，而非单一因素
2. **核心条件**: 低服务质量和高隐私关注是大多数组态中的核心条件
3. **非对称关系**: 导致中辍的因素与影响持续使用的因素存在差异
4. **情境敏感性**: 某些因素的作用效果因用户特征和使用情境而异
5. **实践启示**: 图书馆应针对不同用户群体采取差异化策略
